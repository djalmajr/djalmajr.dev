Dear User,

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application Tesk.

## Information Collected

Our mobile application does not collect any personal or sensitive information about you. We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, location, or any other data that could identify you directly.

## Use of Information

The only information we collect is aggregated and anonymized usage data. This data is used to improve the user experience and for statistical purposes. For example, we may use this data to track which features of the app are used the most or to identify areas where we can improve the user interface.

## Information Sharing

We never share your personal information with anyone else. Aggregated and anonymized usage data may be shared with our business partners, but this data will not identify you in any way.

## Information Security

We take reasonable security measures to protect your information from loss, misuse, unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no security measures are perfect, and we cannot guarantee that your information will never be accessed, used, or disclosed in a breach.

## Your Choices

You can choose to uninstall our mobile application at any time. If you do so, any information we collected about you will be deleted.

## Changes to this Policy

We may change this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make any significant changes, we will notify you by a notice within our mobile application or by email.
